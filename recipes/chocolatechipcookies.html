1.Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Sweet baked treat recognized by its butter flavor along with the inclusion of chocolate chips. Some variations can include nuts, oatmeal, or raisins as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • half tsp salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps/Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

2.Carne Asada

Description

Carne Asada is "grilled meat" in Spanish. However, it has come to connote a particular variety of meals and celebrations throughout many Spanish-speaking cultures. This is one of the most well-known of those meals in this particular dish-marinated, grilled steak that's often served as (the greatest ever) taco filling.

Ingredients

  • Orange Juice
  • Lime Juice
  • Oil
  • Fresh garlic
  • Dried oregano
  • Chili powder
  • Kosher salt
  • Cumin
  • Black pepper
  • Cayenne

Steps/Directions

  1. Marinate your meat. An hour is enough, but no longer than 4 hours to avoid break down of meat.
  2. Season! Sprinkle a little salt and pepper after meat is marinated.
  3. Grill! Usually 5-8 minutes per side.
  4. Let it rest. Do it.
  5. Slice the grilled steak thinly against the grain and serve! Enjoy!
